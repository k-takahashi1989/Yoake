Account deletion request

Use this page to request deletion of your YOAKE account and related data. After we receive your request, we will review it and proceed with account deletion.

What will be deleted

  • Your YOAKE account linked to the submitted email address.
  • Sleep logs, profile settings, reminder settings, and related app data.
  • AI-related records stored as part of your account history.

Some data may be retained temporarily if required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or operational security.

How it works

  • Create a deletion request email from this page.
  • Review the draft and send it.
  • We will verify the request and process the deletion.

Important note

If you only want to sign out from the app, use the logout option in the profile screen. Use this page only when you want to delete the account itself.
